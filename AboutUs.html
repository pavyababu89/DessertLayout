"The dessert house" would be a dedicated establishment for sweet foods and indulgent range of offerings that include brownies, cakes, desserts, chocolates, breads, and savouries.

From our humble beginnings in 2024, when the first The dessert house pastry store opened its doors at the place of Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, India, we've grown to become a Pan-India chain of patisseries with stores in over 10 cities.

Our mission is to spread happiness by serving smiles on a plate, and this journey continues as we open The dessert house patisseries across the country.
